Braun Design
.
Innovative

Act as if there is always a possibility of better.

Useful

Emphasize usefulness, and satisfy functional and aesthetic needs.

Aesthetic

Execute well to be beautiful; our well being depends on it.

Understandable

Clarify structure, and be easy to explain. Be obvious.

Unobstrusive

Stay neutral and restrained, and leave room for self-expression.

Honest

Do not manipulate with promises that cannot be kept.

Long-lasting

Prepare to live many years, so avoid being fashionable.

Detailed

Leave nothing to chance. Be accurate, never arbitrary.

Sustainable

Conserve resources, and reduce visual and physical pollution.

Less, but better

Concentrate on the essential; stay pure, stay simple.

Apple Design

Wayfinding

Be easy to navigate in a hurry.

Feedback

Tell us when something happens or will happen.

Visibility

Make essential functions impossible to miss.

Consistency

Set expectations and stick to them everywhere.

Mental Model

Meet expectations or make new systems simple.

Proximity

Make controls next to what they change.

Grouping

Categorize by function or what makes sense.

Mapping

A slider should go up to make something raise.

Affordance

Indicate how something should be used.

Progressive Disclosure

Start simple; then iteratively go complex.

80/20 Rule

80% benifits from 20% of actions. No clutter.

Symmetry

Optimize for visual harmony.
