Who am I?
Let's start with my heroes.
Rams. Jobs. Jung. Paglia. Ive. Eames. It's a diverse roundup. Rams civilized and brought order to machines. Jobs changed the world by making them friendly. Jung gave us a new ways to understand human personality. Paglia uses it to keep our culture on its toes. Ive made consumer electronics honest and seamless. And the Eames couple changed the way we live.

'Deus ex Machina' refers to when an improbable or unexpected event occurs and resolves a plot or situation. My decision to leave biology and pursue design is one such event. While friends of mine worked on Maleria, I redesigned our research lab. I loved research, but I was always more fascinated about how protocols could get more efficient, accurate, and easy to use.
