Useful
Where does it fit in?

The most useful designs not only work as intended, but they find a snug home in our lives. If it can't decorate our lives for the better, it isn't good design.
Familiar
Have we met?

Familiar design has a comfortable, intuitive feel. It should easily become second nature to use, like riding a bike.

Staying familiar means reinventing the wheel only when necessary.
Magical
Think Disney.

Magical design focuses on the subtle details that bring meaningful delight, creating experiences that might not follow strict logic but feel instinctively right.
On Design
A good design process strives to know the people it's helping just as well as the problems it's solving.

It begins by dreaming up ideal solutions to our challenges and ends by using technology—and the talent of individuals—to bring those dreams into our lives.

The aim is to produce designs that feel familiar yet advanced, elevating human capability through the use of technology and artistic vision.
